## Overview

SpecMon is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected,
how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

## Data collection

SpecMon collects minimal data to understand how the website is used and to improve the user experience.

### Analytics

SpecMon uses **Cloudflare Web Analytics**, a privacy-first analytics service that:

- Does not use cookies or track you across websites
- Does not collect personally identifiable information
- Complies with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR
- Provides aggregated, anonymized metrics only

Learn more about Cloudflare's privacy practices at
[cloudflare.com/privacypolicy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Third-party services

Our website links to and interacts with the following external services:

- **GitHub** - Source code hosting and community discussions
- **Documentation site** (docs.specmon.io) - Technical documentation and guides
- **Academic repositories** - Research papers and artifacts (ACM, arXiv, Zenodo)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data when you visit them.

## Cookies

This website does not use cookies. Cloudflare Web Analytics operates without cookies or local storage.

## Data retention

Analytics data is aggregated and retained according to Cloudflare's data retention policies.
No personally identifiable information is stored.

## Your rights

Since no personally identifiable information is collected, there is no personal data to access,
modify, or delete. If you have concerns about privacy, use the contact information below.

## Contact information

SpecMon is developed and maintained by researchers at the
**CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security**.

For questions, issues, or privacy concerns, visit the
[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/specmon/specmon/issues) page
or start a discussion in
[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/specmon/specmon/discussions).

## Changes to this policy

SpecMon may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page
with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.
